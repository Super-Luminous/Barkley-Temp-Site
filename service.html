CONFIDENT FOUNDATIONS

A six week, one-on-one introduction lifelong grooming success:

  • Week 1: Meet, Greet, Explore
  • Week 2: Handling and Grooming Foundations
  • Week 3: Bathing and Drying Foundations
  • Week 4: Confidence with Grooming Equipment
  • Week 5: Confidence with Grooming Experience
  • Week 6: Graduation Groom

CONSISTENT SUPPORT

For the confident dog:

Price will be based on dog size, coat type and condition

For the fearful dog:

Price will be based on dog size, coat type and condition plus a modified Confident Foundation Plan to acclimatize the dog to grooming

COMMUNITY ASSISTANCE

For the dog awaiting adoption:

A complimentary groom in a low-stress, single dog environment to make a good impression on prospective adopters
A report detailing the dog’s grooming needs will be given to the rescue organization or animal shelter

For the newly adopted dog:

The new pet parents will receive discounted grooms for the first 12 months after adoption
