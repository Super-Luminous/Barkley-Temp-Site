COMING SOON TO WINNIPEG!

Barkley’s Groomer is dedicated to creating a low-stress, wellness-focused grooming experience for small breed dogs and puppies. By combining expert grooming with early behavioural conditioning in a quiet, one on-one environment, we build trust, confidence, and lasting relationships that support dogs throughout every stage of their lives.
